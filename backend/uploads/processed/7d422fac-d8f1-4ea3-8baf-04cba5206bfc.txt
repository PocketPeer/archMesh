E-Commerce Platform Requirements

Business Goals:
- Increase online sales by 40% within the first year
- Reduce customer acquisition cost by 25%
- Improve customer satisfaction scores to 4.5/5
- Expand to international markets within 18 months

Functional Requirements:
- User registration and authentication with email verification
- Social login integration (Google, Facebook)
- Product catalog with search and filtering capabilities
- Shopping cart and checkout functionality
- Multiple payment methods (credit card, PayPal, Apple Pay)
- Order tracking and management system
- Admin panel for product and order management
- Analytics dashboard for business insights

Non-Functional Requirements:
- Performance: Page load times under 2 seconds
- Security: PCI DSS compliance for payment processing
- Scalability: Support 10,000 concurrent users
- Availability: 99.9% uptime requirement
- Mobile responsive design for all devices

Constraints:
- Budget: $500,000 for development
- Timeline: 6 months to launch
- Team: 8 developers maximum
- Technology: Must use cloud infrastructure

Stakeholders:
- CEO: Focus on business growth and ROI
- CTO: Technical architecture and scalability
- Marketing Director: User experience and conversion rates
- Customer Support: Order management and user issues
